Our promise

Free means free. Here's our word.

You've probably seen programmes that promise big and deliver little. So instead of asking for your trust, we'll earn it — starting with what we commit to:

  • Joining costs nothing — no joining fee, no "admin fee", no fees of any kind, ever
  • Money only ever flows TO you — vouchers, tools, and cash milestones you've earned
  • Your details stay yours — we never ask for PINs, passwords or OTPs, and never sell your data
  • Every step is clear — you always know what you're working toward and what it unlocks
  • If anyone asks you for money in our name, it's not us — report it and we'll act

So who pays for all of this? South African companies with turnover above R50 million are required by the B-BBEE Codes to spend a percentage of profits developing small businesses — it's called Enterprise & Supplier Development (ESD). Mzansi Konected delivers that support directly to entrepreneurs, with every rand tracked and reported. The corporates pay us. You never do.
