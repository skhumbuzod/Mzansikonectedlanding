4 · Image Treatment

The photographic voice is set by Nomsa's storyboard: warm, intimate, documentary-real. People first, always — the platform is invisible in photography except as a phone in a hand.

The look

  • Photorealistic, documentary style — real township & small-business settings
  • Warm natural light: golden afternoon sun or warm incandescent evening lamps
  • Intimate, emotional, dignified — joy, focus, pride; never pity
  • Real business textures: vetkoek trays, flour bags, notebooks, card machines, stock shelves
  • The phone/WhatsApp as the bridge — screens visible in-hand, in-context

Never

  • Corporate stock photography — suits, handshakes, glass towers
  • Poverty-porn framing or desaturated "charity appeal" grading
  • Cold blue/grey tech aesthetics — we are warm, human, orange
  • Generic African-market clichés lifted from stock libraries
  • Text-heavy graphics baked into photos
House prompt formula (Midjourney / Firefly):
[warm + dignified subject description, SA-specific] + real business context (township kitchen / spaza / workshop) + device in-hand showing WhatsApp + "warm afternoon light" or "warm incandescent lamp light" + "photorealistic, intimate, emotional documentary photography" + --ar 16:9

Example from the deck: "A warm and joyful middle-aged South African woman in her 50s, wearing a bright floral doek headwrap and apron dusted with flour, sitting at a small wooden table in a sunlit township kitchen, smiling at her Android smartphone reading a WhatsApp message, tray of golden vetkoek beside her. Warm afternoon light, photorealistic, intimate documentary photography."

Grading note: lift warmth (+temp), keep blacks rich (matches #0B0B0B canvas), let orange tones in the scene (doek, vetkoek, sunset) echo Voltage Orange so photos and UI feel like one family.

5 · Voice

Straight talk. Short sentences. Big claims backed by sources. We name the fear before the reader does — then answer it.

"The money exists. The system doesn't."
"We leverage synergies in the ESD ecosystem"
Declarative pairs. Full stops as punctuation of confidence.
"Let's talk straight — is this a scam?"
"We are a trusted and reputable organisation"
Name the doubt first. Trust is earned by candour, not claimed by adjectives.
"We only ever pay money TO you."
"No hidden fees or charges apply"
Concrete direction of money. Green treatment. This line is brand IP — reuse it.
"Prove it. Earn it. Scale it."
"Our gamified learning journey empowers users"
Three verbs beat one buzzword. Rhythm of threes throughout.

6 · Applications

Where each element lives:

  • Pitch deck: black canvas, one giant Lato Black number per slide, orange emphasis, grey source line at the foot. Already the house style — protect it.
  • Landing page: same system (built — mzansikonected-landing.html). Green appears only in the trust/scam section.
  • Email signature: black brand tile + orange divider (built — Arial fallback for email clients).
  • Reward cards / vouchers: alternate black-on-white and white-on-black tiles, orange for the unlock line, "We've got you." in Dongle (per slide 48 of the deck).
  • WhatsApp bot: voice rules apply — short, warm, one emoji max per message, milestone messages get the green treatment in copy ("R2,500 paid ✓").
  • Social: deck-style stat cards travel best — big number, one orange word, source line. Don't post photography without a person in it.
